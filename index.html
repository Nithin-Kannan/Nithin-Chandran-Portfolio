More About Me


Hey there! I'm Nithin Chandran, a Mechanical engineering graudate from TKM Institute Of Technology.

Currently, I’m working as a Graduate Apprentice Trainee at Vikram Sarabhai Space Centre (VSSC) one of the lead centres of the Indian Space Research Organisation (ISRO) located in Thiruvananthapuram, Kerala, India.. I have a keen interest in designing new devices, CAD drawing, project management, and teamwork.

I have previous experience both in industry and research roles. For example, I interned at Maks Automations and that internship provided me with practical experience in automation and robotics. Additionally, at VSSC, The Graduate Apprentice Trainee role has given me invaluable hands-on experience in advanced engineering practices and the opportunity to collaborate with leading experts in the field. Please see my resume (or continue scrolling down here) if you want to learn more about my past positions.

During my time at TKM Institute of Technology, I was actively involved in the National Service Scheme (NSS) and the Innovation and Entrepreneurship Development Cell (IEDC). I served as an Associate Volunteer Secretary in NSS and as CEO of IEDC, where I honed my leadership and organizational skills

In my free time, I enjoy playing Keyboard, making small Projects using Arduino (check out my Experiments for Kerala Iot Challenge here), traveling, solving Rubik's cube, digital drawing,short video editing and watching tech videos :) My passion for creating and tinkering with new ideas extends beyond my professional work, and I love to bring my ideas to life through various DIY projects.

Above all, I am passionate about using my engineering skills to make a positive impact. I believe in the power of technology to solve real-world problems and improve lives. Feel free to explore my portfolio to learn more about my projects and experiences. If you’d like to connect or discuss potential collaborations, please reach out!
